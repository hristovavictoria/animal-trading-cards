North American Raccoon

Raccoons are able to sense objects before touching them using special sensory organs on their claws called vibrissae!

  • Scientific Name: Procyon lotor
  • Average Weight: 5-26 kg
  • Average Lifespan In The Wild: 2-3 years
  • Habitat: Urban and rural environments

The North American raccoon, also called the common raccoon, is a nocturnal omnivore with a striped tail pattern and mask-like fur coloring around the eyes. Females gestate for 60-70 days, usually having from one to seven cubs during early summer.
